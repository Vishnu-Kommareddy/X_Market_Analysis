The financial markets are primarily focused on Federal Reserve Chair Jerome Powell's speech and the anticipated Federal Reserve rate cut, with a 98.3% probability of a cut to 3.75-4.00% by October 29th. This expectation has bolstered safe-haven assets like gold, silver, and cryptocurrencies, as investors seek security amid U.S.-China tensions and the U.S. government shutdown. The crypto market, particularly Bitcoin and Ethereum, is experiencing a positive trend due to the increasing odds of a rate cut. Tariff worries between the U.S. and China have eased, leading to a bounce back in stocks. The upcoming Fed interest rate decision is due on October 29th, and market watchers are eagerly awaiting Powell's announcement. Despite the anticipation, some skepticism exists regarding the potential for a 'sell-the-news' event or future economic issues.
